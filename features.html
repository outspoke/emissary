Plug the mobile gap

50%+ of job seekers visit your jobs on a mobile device. The best ones don't have a resume handy and are not going to peck out an application on a tiny screen... they are going to leave and forget to apply. Emissary closes the loop, bringing them back to apply when the time is right.

Engage more effectively

Manually responding to job seeker inquiries 24/7 would bury your recruiting team, but refusing to engage them drives top talent away before applying. With Emissary, front line communications are managed for you, so your next superstar gets treated like one.

Make collaboration easier

Taking good interview notes is distracting, and sharing them with other hiring team members can be a hassle, but failing to either squanders time and leads to less informed decisions. Emissary takes that burden off of your team and keeps them focused on higher value work.
